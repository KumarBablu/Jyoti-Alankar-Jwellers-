About Us

Every journey has a beginning, Jyoti Alankar Jewellers began in 1986 and grew to be the largest jewellery house with a legacy of over eight decades. The outlet's vision and passion for creating bold and legendary jewellery designs, has been able to carve a way for the company to establish trust amongst its customers for generations now.


The Brand is known for its timeless uniqueness, contemporary designs and exceptional product quality. With the roots in Siwan, Jyoti Alankar Jewellers caters to a diverse category of consumers through its Omnichannel approach.

At Jyoti Alankar Jewellers the artisans craft most iconic pieces of jewellery that beget exclusivity, defining the philosophy that the brand strongly believes in - ‘A jewel of Latest Design’.


By understanding the ever-changing style and preferences of generations, Jyoti Alankar Jwellers focus on constant innovation to provide an unparalleled experience in product design. The designs created by the Brand are indeed beautiful ornaments that not only make a woman look elegant but also make her feel appreciated.
